Amrita School of Engineering

Amrita School of Engineering (ASE) Bangalore campus is one of the five campuses of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University -INDIA. The University holds the No. 1 rank among all private universities in India as per NIRF rankings.

ASE Bangalore campus has two main programs i.e. Engineering and Management Studies. The courses offered include undergraduate, postgraduate as well as doctorate programs.

STUDENT BRANCH

Amrita School of Engineering (ASE) Bangalore campus IEEE Student Branch is one of the very dynamic and active student branches. The current branch counselor is Dr Navin Kumar, an Associate professor in the ECE Dept. of the college. He is also the Chair of ComSoc Bangalore Chapter.
